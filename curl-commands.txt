GET:
curl http://localhost:8000/get?first=John0

GET (search):
curl "http://localhost:8000/search?first=some&last=thing"

POST:
curl -d '{"first": "Homer", "last": "Simpson"}' -H "Content-Type: application/json" -X POST http://localhost:8000/add


PUT:
curl -d '{"first": "Ned", "last": "Flanders"}' -H "Content-Type: application/json" -X PUT http://localhost:8000/update/123
